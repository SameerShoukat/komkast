Frequently Asked Questions

What services does KomKast provide?

We provide WhatsApp Business API access, template management, two-way messaging, campaign dispatch, integrations, and analytics to help businesses automate and measure customer communication.

Is KomKast compliant with WhatsApp policies?

Yes. We assist with template approvals, opt-in tracking, and follow WhatsApp's commerce and messaging policies to keep your account in good standing.

Can you integrate with our CRM?

Absolutely. KomKast supports native integrations, webhooks and custom connectors to sync messages and events with your CRM or backend systems.

What is the typical onboarding time?

Onboarding timelines vary, but most customers go live within 1–3 weeks depending on template approvals and integration complexity.
